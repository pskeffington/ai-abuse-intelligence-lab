# Code of Conduct

## Standard

Participants are expected to communicate professionally, assume good faith, and keep discussion focused on defensive research, reproducibility, and safe engineering.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, doxxing, disclosure of private data, requests for unauthorized access, and attempts to use the project for harmful automation or platform abuse.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, decline pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior conflicts with this standard or the project safety posture.
